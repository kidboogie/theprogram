The Master

Kid Boogie is a world-renowned dancer in the modern Popping scene. Representing the West Coast, he has traveled the globe battling, teaching, and spreading the true essence of Funk styles.

As long time disciple of the original Electric Boogaloos and Pop N Taco, he's known for his hard-hitting style, intricate boogaloo rolls, and musicality. "The Program" is his way of passing down decades of knowledge, ensuring the culture and foundation are preserved for the next generation of dancers.
